PW Skills

PW Skills is an online learning platform that offers courses on various tech skills, including data science, data analytics, artificial intelligence, software engineering, and web development. The courses are taught by industry experts and are designed to help students learn the skills they need to get a job in the tech industry. PW Skills also offers career services to help students with their job search, including resume writing, mock interviews, and access to a database of hiring partners. In addition, PW Skills has a community forum where students can connect with each other and ask questions.

  • Artificial Intelligence Masters
  • Full Stack Web Development
